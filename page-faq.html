Services

Custom Furniture Design

At our store, we specialize in custom furniture design to suit your unique style and space. Whether you're looking for a bespoke sofa set, an elegant dining table, or a custom-made cupboard, our expert designers work closely with you to bring your vision to life. Enjoy personalized service and high-quality craftsmanship tailored to your specific needs.

Furniture Restoration and Repair

Bring new life to your cherished furniture pieces with our restoration and repair services. Our skilled craftsmen are adept at repairing everything from minor scratches to major structural damages, ensuring your furniture retains its beauty and functionality for years to come. We use high-quality materials and techniques to match the original design and finish.

Home Interior Consultation

Transform your home with our expert interior design consultation services. Our team of experienced designers will help you choose the perfect furniture and decor to create a cohesive and stylish look. From selecting the right color palette to optimizing your space, we offer comprehensive guidance to make your home beautiful and functional.

Electrical Works

Our experienced electricians handle all types of electrical works, from installing new wiring and fixtures to repairing and upgrading existing systems. We ensure your home is safe and compliant with the latest standards, providing reliable and efficient electrical solutions.

Plumbing Works

From fixing leaks and unclogging drains to installing new plumbing systems, our skilled plumbers are equipped to handle all your plumbing needs. We offer prompt and professional services to keep your home's water systems running smoothly.

Carpentry

Our carpentry services include custom furniture building, cabinetry, and structural woodwork. Whether you need a new set of shelves, a custom-built wardrobe, or repairs to existing woodwork, our talented carpenters deliver high-quality craftsmanship tailored to your specifications.

Blacksmithing

We offer specialized blacksmithing services for custom metalwork, including gates, railings, and decorative pieces. Our skilled blacksmiths use traditional techniques combined with modern tools to create durable and aesthetically pleasing metalworks that enhance your home's security and style.

Flooring and Tiling

Upgrade your home with our professional flooring and tiling services. We provide expert installation and repair for a variety of materials, including hardwood, laminate, vinyl, and ceramic tiles. Our attention to detail ensures a flawless finish that adds value and beauty to your living spaces.

Painting

Refresh your home with our expert painting services. Our professional painters deliver smooth and even finishes for both interior and exterior surfaces. We use high-quality paints and materials to ensure long-lasting results that enhance your home's appearance.

CCTV Installation and Repair

Ensure the safety and security of your home with our CCTV installation and repair services. Our technicians design and install custom surveillance systems tailored to your specific needs, providing comprehensive coverage and peace of mind. We also offer maintenance and repair services to keep your system operating at peak performance.

Computer Hardware and Software Solutions

Our IT experts provide comprehensive computer hardware and software solutions, including installation, repair, and upgrades. Whether you need to set up a new system, troubleshoot issues, or enhance your current setup, we offer reliable and efficient services to meet all your technology needs.
